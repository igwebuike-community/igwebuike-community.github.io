Joining our Igwebuike community

Belonging to Igwebuike provides you with ample opportunities to do the following:
  1. Be a key participant in the “Igbo Language and New Technologies” history, which is currently in-the-making;
  2. Be among people with shared vision and mission;
  3. Be the first to be notified about opportunities from all around the world;
  4. Our Igwebuike community is a pool of diverse people with different talents, who might be contacted in the future for collaborations and networking;
  5. To have your name (and personal details/businesses) advertised in the Igwebuike Hall of Fame (click here to see the requirements that will help you qualify for this great opportunity).

Igwebuike Community Group

Join our Igwebuike Community Group if you are:
  • Interested in being a part of the general Igwebuike community group;
  • As a member, you can participate in our group meetings and discussions;
  • You will have ample opportunities to record and validate our sentence collections;
  • You stand the chance of being showcased in our Igwebuike Hall of Fame;
  • You can be contacted for possible collaborations on other projects;
  • You will have ample opportunities to interact with Ndigbo that live outside Nigeria;
  • Be among Umunne and Umunna from different parts of the world.
