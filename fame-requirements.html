Requirements for the Igwebuike Hall of Fame

The Igwebuike Hall of Fame is an online platform where names and brief details (including names of businesses / homes / villages / schools) of successful Igwebuike participants are advertised on the Igwebuike Community Page.

In order to be recognized as a successful Igwebuike participant, thus, qualifying for your details to be on the Igwebuike Hall of Fame, an intending participant is expected to log into the Mozilla Common Voice platform and complete the following:
  1. Record at least 100 sentences (see how to record your voice in simple steps).
  2. Validate at least 100 recorded voices (see how to validate recorded voices).
  3. In addition to 1 & 2 above, try to get at least 3 people to record.
